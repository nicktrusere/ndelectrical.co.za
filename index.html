ELECTRICAL ENGINEERS DIVISION

Our certified electrical engineers provide cutting-edge solutions for complex industrial and commercial power systems across South Africa:

  • Advanced power system design for factories, hospitals, and commercial buildings
  • Energy efficiency audits to reduce operational costs by up to 40%
  • Industrial automation with PLC and SCADA systems integration
  • GMR compliance (General Machinery Regulations) certification
  • Hazardous location electrical system design
  • Renewable energy integration (Solar/Wind)

Certifications & Compliance

ND Electrical is registered with the Electrical Contracting Board of South Africa (ECB) and is a member of professional bodies including the Electrical Contractors Association (ECA), South African Flameproof Association (SAFA), and the South African Electric Fence Installers Association (SAEFIA). Our work adheres to standards such as SANS 10142 and SANS 10089, ensuring safety, professionalism, and quality in every project.
